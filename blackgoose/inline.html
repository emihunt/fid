Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide.
In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath thatis precisely set to the target tempperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
